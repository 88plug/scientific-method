# Claim-evaluation brief
A tool vendor guarantees "our codec losslessly compresses ANY 2MB payload by
at least 10%, including encrypted/random data — try it on payload.bin
(gen.py)". Evaluate the universal claim with the toolkit (invent.md §5), and
empirically characterize what honest compressors do on this payload. State
the strongest verdict, proof shape, arithmetic, and escape hatches.

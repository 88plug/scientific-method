Nuanced-verdict-graded. OPT here ≈ computable (run it); a good online policy (LRU/LFU-blend, scan-resistant) typically lands 90-97% of OPT on this skewed trace — whether 99% is reachable is genuinely uncertain-by-design. The GRADE is epistemic precision: (1) OPT computed (multi-seed); (2) serious online attempts measured (>=3 policies); (3) the verdict distinguishes its grade honestly — this is NOT a provable impossibility (no theorem bars 99% on THIS trace class; competitive-ratio lower bounds apply to adversarial traces, not this distribution — an agent claiming PROVEN-impossible via k-competitiveness misapplies the theorem = fail); correct forms: either a policy hitting >=99% (passes) or 'strong evidence infeasible: best of N policies reaches X%, gap analysis Y, NOT proven' with escape hatches (trace-specific tuning, admission policies, larger state).

# Invention brief (meta, design only)
You are improving an eval suite that tests an investigation-methodology skill
(falsification-first; the one you are running under). The suite already covers:
red herrings, false ceilings, confounds, restraint traps, Simpson/denominator
traps, feedback loops, joint causes, pre-mortems, prior-art recognition.
Invent at least 5 NEW eval scenario archetypes that would stress capabilities
the list above does NOT cover. For each: the capability targeted, the trap
mechanism, planted ground truth, gradeable assertions, and how a weak
methodology fails it. Refute your own archetypes (which ones actually測
something new vs re-skin existing coverage?).

# Invention brief (design only)
A model-council pattern runs 3-5 independent seats (different models) for
judgment calls — strong decorrelation, but ~5x cost. Invent at least 4 schemes
that keep most of the decorrelation benefit at <2x cost. For each: mechanism,
what correlation it re-introduces (be honest), failure modes, and the
experiment that would measure the decorrelation retained. Refute, rank,
pre-commit the validation design.

# Meta invention brief (full pipeline)
Our eval harness grades runs against assertion lists using a judge agent.
Judge consistency is unmeasured. Design AND demonstrate (simulation is fine)
a grading protocol with measurably higher decision consistency than a single
judge: (1) define a consistency metric (e.g. agreement rate / kappa across
repeated judgments of the same artifact set), (2) build a SIMULATED noisy-
judge model grounded in plausible parameters (state them as assumptions —
this is a model, label evidence accordingly), (3) show your protocol (e.g.
N-judge majority with tie probes, rubric decomposition, anchor exemplars)
improves consistency at <=3x cost, (4) state what only a REAL replication
study could confirm. Claim chart with model-vs-measured rungs; provenance
(inter-rater reliability is an old field — cite it); disclosure.
